About us

Searching for a property is a complex process with a lot of uncertainty and with lack of transparency. Our vision is to shed some light in the darkness,and provide a powerful solution for property search in Greece with the use of technology!

vriskospiti.gr is Greece's No 1 real estate website, with more than 750,000 properties to buy or rent, available to over 1.5 million visitors generating 54 million page views a month.

After being founded in 2006, vriskospiti.gr was the first Greek online startup to raise growth capital from venture capital fund Oxygen Capital in 2009, and since 2014 has become part of Real Web, which controls Immobiliare.it, the No 1 real estate website in Italy.

vriskospiti.gr is part of vriskospti.gr Network and together with Spiti24.gr, Tospitimou.gr and vriskospiti.com.cy, they constitute the largest real estate database in Greece and Cyprus. In 2017, vriskospiti.gr Network created the brand Indomio and expanded its presence in foreign countries by launching new international property websites. With more than 40 million visits and one billion page-views per year, vriskospiti.gr Network reinforces the vision of upgrading property search, by offering the ideal solution for users who are searching and for professionals who want to stand out worldwide.

Over 90% of the available properties for sale in Greece are listed on vriskospiti.gr Network, a total portfolio worth around € 250 billion in value based on the asking price.
